Learn to drive with confidence

Hi, I’m Kelly. I’m a calm and patient driving instructor based in Buxton, Derbyshire, with over 30 years of experience behind the wheel. As an automatic driving specialist, I help learners of all ages feel at ease — whether you’re a complete beginner or returning to driving later in life.

I understand that learning to drive can feel nerve-wracking, so I focus on keeping lessons relaxed, clear, and encouraging. My aim is to help you feel comfortable behind the wheel while building the skills you need to pass your test and drive independently.

Lessons take place in a modern Volkswagen T-Cross (2026 plate), giving you a comfortable, reliable car to learn in.

Driving lessons tailored to you

Beginner lessons

Perfect if you’re starting from scratch. We’ll begin with the basics and build your confidence step by step.

Refresher lessons

Ideal if you haven’t driven in a while or want to rebuild confidence before getting back on the road.

Test preparation

Focused lessons to get you ready for your driving test, including mock tests and route practice.

Nervous driver support
